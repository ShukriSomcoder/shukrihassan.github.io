Creating classroom displays

Classroom displays are an integral part of the classroom.

Enabling Environments

Classrooom displays help to create enabling evironments as they hold information about topics, vocabulary and even celebrate learning.

Displaying words allows the learning environment to become print rich, allowsing children to access words and phrases that they can use in their own writing.

When children have access or are in an environment whic is anabling it can improve the quality of their work as they have good quality examples to use as a scaffold or guide for writing.

Interactive displays

Creating displays that support learning areas in the classroom support childrens independent learning. Creating interactive displays such as this allows children to engage with the environment and access different resources allowing them to develop the knowledge and skills.

Documenting learning

Good quality learning: Displays are a key place to display good quality examples of work for example the voacbulary needed for the topic and work related to the class topic.

Inclusion: It is important to display a work from children with a range of abilities as this shows that children with learning difficulties are still valued within the class.

Learning Journey: Displays can also be &live. For example working walls that document the learning taking place during each lesson and is updated regulary to suppoty the class'learning journey.
